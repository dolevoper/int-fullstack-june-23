my journey


It all starts when i was 21
It was a normal day, I went to work and when I returned home I started having severe pain,
when I went to the doctor he repeatedly said it was a pelvic infection [more information]
click hello
and not something serious but I knew something was wrong in my body. I insisted and so they sent me for tests to find out what the problem was.
After a few days of receiving the answers, the doctor told me that I have a disease called AS or in Hebrew stiffening vertebrae.
I didn't know what awaited me and what I was facing,
but the doctor reassured me that there was treatment and that it was possible to live a completely normal life alongside the disease.
and so it was. The first days in dealing with the disease were difficult not only physically but mentally.
Digesting the situation was strange and difficult at the same time.
At first I didn't understand what I was supposed to do
if I was supposed to change my behavior I was supposed to sit at home and suffer whether I could go out and behave like a normal person before the pain.
The months passed and that's how I researched the disease,
what could be done in addition to medication.
I entered every possible forum, every group on Facebook and thus I discovered a whole world of people who suffer from similar diseases,
each of whom is a whole world that helps and supports the "newcomers".
After a lot of questions, searches, doubts and pain,
I discovered that along with the medication, I would also have to start exercising regularly in order to reduce the pain, and that's how it really was.

The first trainings were difficult, the pains were unbearable
but little by little the pains subsided and I started to return to the life before
but along with the medicines and the trainings.
I had to accept the fact that I live alongside a chronic illness, medication and non-stop training in order to live a "normal life".
