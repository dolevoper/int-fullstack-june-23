About Us

The Israeli Air Force (IAF) is an integral part of the Israel Defense Forces (IDF) and serves as the country's aerial arm. Established in 1948, the IAF has been tasked with defending the Israeli airspace, conducting combat sorties, and providing support to ground forces. With a wide range of advanced aircraft and cutting-edge technologies, the IAF has participated in numerous operations and played a crucial role in Israel's defense.

The IAF operates a diverse fleet of aircraft, including advanced fighter jets like the F-16 and F-35, versatile attack helicopters such as the Apache, reconnaissance aircraft, transport planes, and various other aerial platforms. These aircraft are manned by highly skilled pilots and supported by dedicated technicians and maintenance crews. The IAF maintains a high level of readiness with aircraft on constant standby, performing daily operations, training exercises, search and rescue missions, and air defense tasks.
